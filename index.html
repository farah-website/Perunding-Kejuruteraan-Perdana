Company Overview

Established in 1995

Perunding Kejuruteraan Perdana (PKP) is a trusted and experienced engineering consultancy firm based in Malaysia. We specialize in Civil & Structural (C&S) as well as Mechanical & Electrical (M&E) engineering solutions, delivering projects that range from educational institutions to high-rise developments.

Our Vision

To be a leading engineering consultancy firm known for quality, integrity, and excellence in delivering innovative and sustainable engineering solutions.

Our Mission

To provide professional engineering services that exceed client expectations, uphold safety standards, and contribute positively to the built environment.
